Marketing Intern

Overview: Finanta is at the forefront of revolutionizing commercial lending, offering a unique platform tailored to meet the needs of banks, credit unions, and financial institutions. Our innovative approach combines advanced digital integration with precise data-driven decisioning. We are seeking a motivated Product Marketing and Growth Intern to join our dynamic team and contribute to our continued success.

Duration: This is a paid internship, requiring a commitment of at least 6 months.

Responsibilities:

  • Pursuing or holding an undergraduate degree in Marketing, Business Administration, Communications, or a related field; an MBA is advantageous.

  • Demonstrated experience in digital marketing, including knowledge of free and paid channels.

  • Proficiency in marketing tools, including Adobe Creative Suite, and familiarity with Google Analytics and Tag Manager.

  • Strong analytical skills, with a focus on market research and competitive analysis.

  • Excellent communication skills, creativity, and problem-solving capabilities.

  • Proficiency in English; additional language skills are a plus.

  • Demonstrated ability to work autonomously and collaboratively.

  • Previous experience with CRM and email marketing tools is preferred.

  • Portfolio submission is encouraged, showcasing relevant work.

Learning and Development:

  • Gain practical experience in product marketing and growth strategy within the fintech sector.

  • Acquire knowledge in regulatory compliance and digital marketing techniques.

  • Receive mentorship from experienced marketing professionals in a supportive learning environment.

Company Culture:

  • At Finanta, innovation, continuous learning, and collaboration are the pillars of our work culture.

  • We are dedicated to fostering diversity, inclusion, and a balanced approach to work-life integration.

Benefits:

  • Flexible work-from-home options.

  • Comprehensive training and development opportunities.

  • A certificate upon successful completion of the internship.

  • Potential for a full-time position for outstanding performers.
